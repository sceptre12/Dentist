Who we are

Dr. Todd Botwin earned his DMD degree from Tufts University College of Dental Medicine in Boston. He holds a Bachelor’s of Science degree from Alfred University in Alfred NY. He has practiced in New Jersey for fourteen years before coming to Florida. He was an associate professor at Nova Southeastern before starting his practice here in Boynton Beach.

Dr. Maria Zecca-Botwin earned her DMD at Tufts University College of Dental Medicine in Boston. She holds a Bachelor of Science degree from St. Peter’s College (University) in Jersey City. She also practiced in New Jersey for fourteen years before coming to Boynton Beach in 2005.

Dr. B and Dr. Z have been married for 23 years and have three children. They are also parents of four dogs. They love working together as much as they love their family and patients. Their hobbies include relaxing in Key Largo, boating, biking, gardening and yoga.

Comfortable Care & Brighter Smiles

The doctors and staff of Botwin Family Dental Care welcome you to our website. Dr. Todd Botwin and his wife, Dr. Maria Zecca-Botwin have taken dental care to the highest level in their expertise and exceptional patient communication skills. Our staff is caring and accommodating to our patients. Our goal is to provide all of our patients with as much information on their dental health, prevention of dental disease and a treatment plan that is manageable and realistic. Our patients find themselves walking in as a stranger, but leaving as a friend, even after the first visit.
